Weightless




Who are we?

Weightless is an organization founded by students Hyuntae Kim and Gilberto Rogel. We came up with this idea because of all the people we see in our population most are overweight or have obesity, which is a problem of high magnitude in our society.



Mission

Offer a free opportunity for those who want to loose weight by changing their daily exercise routine and providing a good diet in order to aquire the ideal weight, according to the chart shown below.



Vision

Decrease the user's bodyweight.
