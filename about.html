Which problem are we trying to solve?

The United States of America along with Mexico are the countries with the highest rate of obesity in their citizens and according to the Poll of Health and Nutrition in 2012, half of the population is overweight.


What is obesity?

Obesity is a medical condition in which excess body fat has accumulated to the extent that it may have a negative effect on health. It is most commonly caused by a combination of excessive food intake, lack of physical activity, and genetic susceptibility.
